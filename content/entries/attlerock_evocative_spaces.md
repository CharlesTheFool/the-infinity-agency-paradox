### The Evocative Contract

Evocative spaces make a deal with explorers: I'll provide environmental detail rich with implication. You provide the knowledge framework to decode it into story. The Nomai never explain obvious connections. they trust you'll make the conceptual leaps. Different explorers construct different narratives from identical spaces based on their external knowledge.

### Connection to Epistemic Agency

Evocative spaces create interpretation agency: you decide what the environment means based on your framework. Two explorers can stand in the same location and construct different narratives. The environment provides facts. You provide meaning. The gap between fact and meaning is where epistemic agency lives.
